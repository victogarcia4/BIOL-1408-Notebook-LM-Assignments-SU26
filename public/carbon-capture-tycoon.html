Win Condition

To make 1 G3P in this game, collect enough energy and fix enough carbon:

  • At least 6 ATP
  • At least 4 NADPH
  • At least 3 CO2 fixed
When you have enough resources, build G3P.

Reflection

This game teaches that the light reactions and the Calvin Cycle are connected. The light reactions make ATP and NADPH. The Calvin Cycle uses those molecules to help turn CO2 into sugars. Rubisco begins the carbon fixation step by attaching carbon dioxide to RuBP.
